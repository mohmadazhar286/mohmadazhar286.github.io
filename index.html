Profile

I move between research, code, training, and academic systems.

My research began with a simple question: how much of a person's behavioral pattern can be understood from the way they use language? That question led me into automatic personality recognition, psycholinguistic features, deep multi-label classification, graph neural networks, and transformers.

I also build things that have to work outside a paper. At SKUAST Kashmir, as a MANAGE Fellow and web developer for HADP 20, I am involved in conceiving and implementing digital systems for agricultural research and extension workflows.

I do not separate teaching from engineering. I use the same habit in both: understand the idea, build the working version, explain the tradeoffs, and make the system or student stronger by the end of the process.
